Maybe too late, but hope still useful.

In AWS Cost Explorer > Explore Cost & Usage, filter by service and select EC2-Other.
In the graph, group by "usage type".

Among others, you will have the list of costs for the following services:
  • USW2-EBS:VolumeUsage.gp2
  • USW2-EBS:SnapshotUsage
  • USW2-ElasticIP:IdleAddress
  • USW2-DataTransfer-Regional-Bytes
  • USW1-EBS:VolumeUsage
  • Others
For the detailed list, look at the list below the graph.

Marko.
